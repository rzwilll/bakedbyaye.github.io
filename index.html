ABOUT

During the summer of 2020, Noroul Ain Bangkero Ali, a student who loves to bake, experimented her own ways of making her own low-cost New York Style Chocolate Chip Cookies without depreciating the quality. With her great determination, she managed to establish her own little business and sold almost three hundred boxes of cookies within five months of business operation.

Baked by Aye’s cookies are homemade and most certainly, baked with love and passion.
